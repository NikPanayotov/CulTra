The Binary Buzzer Language

Can you understand and use a language made up of only two sounds?

⬇⬆

Thanks for considering participation in our language learning game! We are really glad you are here!

Our research group is trying to understand whether humans can learn a very simple language consisting of only two sounds - a high tone and a low tone.

Turns out this is not that easy so to better understand why we are trying to track the learning process of many participants.

To do so, it is really important for us that once you start you continue until you reach the learning goal which will be assigned to you.

It may take between 15 to 30 min or even a bit more depending on the learning conditions you get in the game.

This is not a reflection of your abilities and we are very grateful for your persistence.

You can track your scores throughout the game to see how far you are from reaching your assigned goal.

Please try to find a quiet place to avoid distractions during the session.
