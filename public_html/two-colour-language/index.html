Two Colour Language

Can you understand and use a language expressed with only two colours?

●●

Scientists are trying to understand whether humans could communicate using only two colour signals.

See how well you can understand and use such a language! It may take between 15 and 30 min or even a bit more depending on the learning conditions you get in the game.

This is not a reflection of your abilities and we are very grateful for your persistence.

Please try to find a quiet place to avoid distractions during that time.

Thank you for helping our research!
